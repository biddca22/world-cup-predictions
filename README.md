# world-cup-predictions
Predictive modelling of 2002-2022 FIFA World Cup matches with pre-match and in-match data. Includes outcome prediction, win-probability estimation, and statistical analysis.

## Data Sources

* Alex. (2024). *FIFA World Ranking* [Data set]. Kaggle. [https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/cashncarry/fifaworldranking](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/cashncarry/fifaworldranking)

* Fjelstul, J. C. (2023). *The Fjelstul World Cup Database* (v.1.2.0) [Data set]. GitHub. [https://www.github.com/jfjelstul/worldcup](https://www.github.com/jfjelstul/worldcup)

* goto_conversion. (2021). *FIFA World Cup Match Stats* [Data set]. Kaggle. [https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/kaito510/fifa-world-cup-match-stats/data](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/kaito510/fifa-world-cup-match-stats/data)

* Hersbach, H., et al. (2023). *ERA5 hourly data on single levels from 1940 to present* [Data set]. ECMWF. [https://doi.org/10.24381/cds.adbb2d47](https://doi.org/10.24381/cds.adbb2d47)

* Iron486. (2022). *FIFA World Cup 2022: Complete Dataset* [Data set]. Kaggle. [https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/die9origephit/fifa-world-cup-2022-complete-dataset](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/die9origephit/fifa-world-cup-2022-complete-dataset)

* Tripathi, S. (2022). *FIFA World Cup 2022 Match Data* [Data set]. Kaggle. [https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/swaptr/fifa-world-cup-2022-match-data](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/swaptr/fifa-world-cup-2022-match-data)

* Zippenfenig, P. (2023). *Open-Meteo.com Weather API* [Computer software]. Zenodo. [https://doi.org/10.5281/ZENODO.7970649](https://doi.org/10.5281/ZENODO.7970649)
